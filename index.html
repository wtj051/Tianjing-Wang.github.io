Research agenda

Safe grid operation

Physics-guided learning, graph/time-series models, reinforcement learning, and optimization for power-flow feasibility, voltage/security constraints, emergency control, and resilient operation under uncertainty.

Life-aware battery intelligence

SOH/SOC/RUL estimation, future-capacity prediction, degradation-aware dispatch, and health-to-value decision tools for grid-edge storage, EVs, and battery systems.

Collaborative energy intelligence

Federated learning, privacy-preserving coordination, multi-agent decision systems, and big-small model architectures for distributed vehicles, charging stations, storage assets, aggregators, and utilities.

Featured public benchmark

TrustEnergyBench

TrustEnergyBench is a public benchmark for evaluating AI agents and foundation-model components in critical energy systems. It combines public/synthetic grid scenarios, EV/storage uncertainty, and public battery degradation datasets to test physical feasibility, robustness, privacy-aware coordination, and long-term battery impact.

Initial tracks: SafeFlex, BatteryLife, and CollaborativeEnergy.
